About Projeto Desenvolve

Projeto Desenvolve is an educational platform designed to empower aspiring web developers. Our mission is to provide a hands-on learning experience where students can practice, build, and deploy their own web applications.

Why Choose Projeto Desenvolve?

  • Free access to powerful learning tools and resources.
  • 500MB of storage and 500MB MySQL databases for each project.
  • Custom subdomains to showcase your work to the world.
  • Learn by doing with real-world projects and professional guidance.

What Can You Build?

With Projeto Desenvolve, you can create a wide range of web applications, including:

  • Personal portfolios and blogs
  • E-commerce websites
  • Content management systems (CMS)
  • Social media platforms
